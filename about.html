    About Us

    Located inside UST, Cozy Nook offers a relxing spot inside the campus where students, faculty, and visitors can come to enjoy to relaxing atmosphere. We also serve a variety of products, from different types of beverages, meals, pastries, and cakes.


    Mission

    To provide students, faculty, and staff with nourishing, affordable, and thoughtfully crafted meals in a welcoming environment that inspires connection, and comfort.

    Vision

    To become the campus’ favorite gathering space—recognized for exceptional service, a warm and inclusive atmosphere, and a commitment to quality food that supports both well-being and academic success.
